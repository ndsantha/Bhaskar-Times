IND A vs PAK A Live, Emerging Asia Cup final: Tahir slams brilliant century

India A vs Pakistan A Live Score, Emerging Teams Asia Cup final: Pakistan A openers, Saim Ayub (59) and Sahibzada Farhan (65) forged a 121-run stand for the opening wicket after Yash Dhull invited the side to bat first in Colombo. The partnership was broken by Manav Suthar, and the dismissal triggered a middle-order collapse with Omair Yousuf (35), Qasim Akram (0), and skipper Mohammad Haris (2) departing in quick succession as well. Riyan Parag took two in two overs and it seemed India are making inroads, but Tayyib Tahir brought Pakistan A back in the game with some brilliant bits of counter-attacking. He raced to his half-century in 42 balls and took on the India A bowlers, smashing them all around the park.
